How To Care For Your Flowers

  • Clean the vase thoroughly with soapy water to remove any bacteria.
  • Keep fresh flowers away from extreme temperatures, direct sunlight and ripening fruit.
  • Most flowers will last longer under cool conditions.
  • Keep your vase filled with clean water.
  • Remove dead or wilting leaves and stems.
  • Remove any leaves under water to prevent bacteria.
  • Cut stems with a sharp knife at an angle about one to two inches from the bottom every few days.
